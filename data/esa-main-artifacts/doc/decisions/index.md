# Energy System Architecture - Decision Records

This directory contains Architectural Decision Records (ADRs) for the Energy System Architecture team at Alliander.

## About ADRs

ADRs document significant architectural decisions made during the design and development of energy systems. Each ADR captures:
- The context and problem being addressed
- The decision made
- The consequences and trade-offs

## Document Format

All ADRs follow the standard ADR format with sections for:
- Status (proposed, accepted, deprecated, superseded)
- Context
- Decision
- Consequences
