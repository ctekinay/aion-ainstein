# Energy System Architecture - Principles

This directory contains Architecture Principles for the Energy System Architecture team at Alliander.

## About Principles

Architecture Principles establish the fundamental guidelines and rules that govern the design, development, and operation of energy systems. They provide:
- Consistent direction for architectural decisions
- Alignment with organizational goals and standards
- Foundation for evaluating design alternatives
