# AInstein Artifacts Repository

This repository hosts artifacts for development and additional enabling architecture in the Alliander enterprise. It supports the (Energy System) Architecture in delivering tailored architecture-related answers to various stakeholders. The approach is based on shared common knowledge and emphasizes human-in-the-loop principles to ensure effective and personalized communication.

## Purpose

The primary goal of this repository is to empower architecture groups (at this moment limited to ESA) to provide architecture insights that are:
- Tailored to different types of stakeholders
- Based on shared and common knowledge
- Delivered with human oversight and involvement

As a result, stakeholders will be positively surprised by:
- The effectiveness of communication around their topics
- The relevance and precision of the deliverables
- The impact of the architecture team’s output

## General goals

- Enable ESA groups to answer architecture-related topics in a stakeholder-specific manner
- Maintain human involvement while leveraging shared knowledge
- Improve stakeholder engagement and satisfaction through tailored communication

## Architecture group-Goals

- **Efficiency Improvement**: Create time in the team by improving efficiency and avoiding unnecessary stakeholder management through one-time-hit communication.
- **Contextual Flexibility**: Enable answering the same content in different contexts (e.g., transitioning from departmental to chain-based organization).
- **Enhanced Impact**: Create more impact with architecture through better and tailor-made deliverables.
- **Data Control**: Start learning from a point where we are in control of the data itself (e.g., architecture files and related artifacts).

## Governance

Currently, governance is limited to the ESA group and any architects or contributors actively wishing to contribute. Upon delivery, governance will be updated accordingly to reflect broader participation and oversight.

## Contribution

Contributions are welcome from architects and other stakeholders who are actively engaged in the architecture process. Please follow the contribution guidelines and ensure alignment with the repository’s goals and governance model.
